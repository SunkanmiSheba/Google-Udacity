Quokka

Quokkas feed at night on grass, leaves and bark. They also have the ability to survive for a long time without food or water, using the fat stored in their tails.

  • Scientific Name: Setonix brachyurus
  • Average Length: 40-54 centimeters
  • Average Lifespan: 10 years
  • Habitat: It survives in large numbers in the seasonally arid and harsh environment of Rottnest Island
